# Celestial Events Reference

- **Halley''s Comet Return (1531)** - Visible across the Southern Hemisphere; the aged chasqui witnesses it over Chucuito, reading it as the long-awaited *pachakuti* omen amid smallpox and civil war murmurs.
- **Halley''s Comet Anticipation (1452)** - Young chasqui hears astronomer-priests forecast the comet''s next appearance, embedding expectation and ritual preparation into his early routes.
- **Annular Solar Eclipse (1492)** - Path arcs across present-day Costa Rica, Panama, Colombia, and Brazil; unseen by the chasqui but revealed to the reader as a hidden sign that the world is turning.
- **Halley''s Comet Return (1986)** - Future archaeologist, then a young observer, watches the comet over Lake Titicaca, imprinting awe and cosmic curiosity.
- **Lake Titicaca Meteor Shower (1992)** - As an archaeologist, she witnesses a vivid meteor display and recalls the 1986 comet, linking modern fieldwork to ancestral skywatching.
